KELASKU

We provide classes or workshops for those of you who wish to learn about Nail care and Nail Art. You will be coached by professional Nail care experts and Nail artists from all over the world.

KELASKU is perfect for individuals interested in learning more about the exciting and challenging careers available for nail technicians. Whether you'd like to become a nail artist or simply just curious about the nail industry, this course will help anyone to gain fundamental skills and various advanced techniques.
